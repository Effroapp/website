Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Storage, recall, AI summaries, and automatic tool integration — designed to give you complete clarity on any area, without adding to the noise.

🗂

Areas & Threads

Each spinning plate gets an area. Break it into threads of focused work. Keep a chronological record of todos, decisions, meetings, and notes — all in one searchable place.

⚡

Smart Generate

Drop in an email, calendar invite, or PDF. Effro extracts the structured items for you to approve or refine. No manual copy-paste required.

📡

Signals

Outlook and Jira feed you automatically every 30 minutes. New meetings and issues land in Signals for triage. Decide what's worth filing and where.

🔭

Insights

Glance-and-go view. Your most and least active areas, upcoming meetings, and due todos — surfaced without digging through anything. Read it in under a minute.

🧠

AI Overview + Roundup

Area summaries regenerate daily so you can step back into any project without re-reading everything. Weekly Roundup drafts your status digest in one click.

🔒

Private by default

Desktop app. Your database and uploads stay on your machine. No Effro cloud, no subscription, no tracking. Optionally connect Nextcloud for encrypted backups.

How it works

Three steps to staying
across everything

01

Set up your areas

Each responsibility you're carrying gets an area — one per spinning plate. Client projects, internal work, personal admin. Effro starts empty and shapes itself around you.

02

Let things arrive

Connect Outlook and Jira. Meetings and issues arrive in Signals for triage. Drop emails and PDFs into Smart Generate. Log todos, decisions, and notes as they happen.

03

Stay oriented

Daily nudges, AI-refreshed area summaries, and Insights keep you oriented — without you needing to hold it all in your head. That's Effro's job, not yours.

Designed with you in mind

Built for how your mind
actually works

Most productivity tools were designed for an idealised version of attention that very few people actually have. They badge, remind, and nag — adding to the cognitive load they promised to reduce. Effro was built differently, with attention regulation and executive function front of mind.

🔇

No noise

No notifications. No badge counts. No streak guilt. No morning nudges. Effro never competes for your attention — it waits for you, and everything is exactly where you left it.

⚡

Executive function support

The system does the remembering, sequencing, and context-holding that executive function demands. Starting a thread, finding a decision, recalling where you are — all frictionless, one click.

🌿

Permission to switch off

When the day ends, you can genuinely close the laptop. Everything is stored, summarised, and ready. The moment you return, one click brings you fully back up to speed on any area.

Whether you are neurodivergent, managing high cognitive load, or simply tired of systems that demand more attention than they give back — Effro works with you in harmony, not against you.

Structure

Areas → Threads → Entries

A clear hierarchy that maps to how professional context actually accumulates — not a blank page, not another wiki.

Each area is a plate you're spinning. Inside it, threads are the individual strands of work. Inside each thread, entries are the atomic record — a todo, a decision, a meeting note, a blocker, anything worth capturing.

Entry types carry status through the system. Todos track completion. Decisions are logged with context. Meetings link to calendar events. Blockers surface in the dashboard.
