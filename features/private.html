The promise

Four plain promises,
no fine print

Privacy you can state in a sentence each, because it is built into how Effro works, not bolted on as a policy.

Your data stays on your device.

Effro stores everything locally, your database and your file uploads alike. Nothing syncs to a server we run. No one else can read it, not even us.

Bring your own AI.

Use Claude, Groq, Gemini, or a fully local model with Ollama. Your key, your model, your data, never used to train anyone's system.

Back up on your terms.

Backups are optional and off by default. Turn on encrypted Nextcloud backup and your safety net lives where you choose, not on our infrastructure.

No cloud. No subscription.

Effro is free to download and use. No account to create, no recurring bill, no lock-in. You pay your AI provider directly, only for what you use.

Bring your own AI
