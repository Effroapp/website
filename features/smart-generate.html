How it works

Stop turning your inbox
into a to-do list by hand

The cost of staying organized is rarely the work itself. It is the constant copy, paste, and reword to get it into a system. Smart Generate does that part, so you only ever do the deciding.

Paste the chaos. Get clean items.

Forward yourself a thread, paste a brain-dump, or drop in raw meeting notes. Smart Generate reads through it and returns a short list of structured items, each tagged as a todo, decision, meeting, or note, with a one-line reason it was pulled out so you can trust what it found.

"Caught up with Sarah, we agreed to ship the API v2 redesign in Q3. I need to send the scope doc by Friday, and book the review..."
DecisionShip API v2 redesign in Q3
TodoSend scope doc by Friday

Any file, one inbox.

It is not just text. Drag a PDF, an email (.eml), or a calendar invite (.ics) straight onto the panel and Effro parses it first, then extracts from the contents. A meeting invite even comes back as a meeting item with the date and time already set.

It files itself into the right place.

Every item is matched against the areas and threads you already have. If an item belongs in an open thread, Smart Generate selects it. If nothing fits, it suggests a sensible new thread. You are never sorting from scratch, just confirming where things land.

You stay in control.

Nothing is saved until you say so. Approve an item and it lands in your log. Not quite right? Reject it with a reason and Smart Generate refines just that one item from your feedback. When a batch looks good, approve them all at once.
